About

The Department of Mechanical Science and Engineering Rapid Prototyping Lab was made possible by a substantial gift of $750,000 by the Ford Motor Company in 1995. The purpose of the MechSE RP Lab is to allow students access to current manufacturing technologies in their course work, independent projects, and research. The RP Lab is used heavily by courses in the engineering mechanics and mechanical engineering curriculum, including:

ME 170: Computer-Aided Design
 ME 270: Design for Manufacturability
 ME 370: Mechanical Design I
 ME 370: Mechanical Design II
 ME 470: Senior Design Project

How To Print a Part

1. Create a STL

Using your favorite CAD software output your part as a STL (STereoLithography) file.

2. Get a Quote

Upload your STL file for a quote on how much your part will cost to print.

3. Create a Ticket

Upload your file and generate a ticket to put your part in the queue.

4. Pick it Up

Come claim your part at the lab or we can ship it to you!
