About

The MechSE Rapid Prototyping Lab was made possible by a substantial gift of $750,000 by the Ford Motor Company in 1995. The purpose of the MechSE RP Lab is to allow students access to current manufacturing technologies in their course work, independent projects, and research. The RP Lab is equipped with a number of systems, including CNC milling and turning centers; a wire EDM machine; and three rapid prototyping systems.

How To

1. Create an STL

Using your favorite CAD software output your part as a STL (STereoLithography) file.

2. Get a Quote

Upload your STL file for a quote on how much your part will cost to print.

3. Create a Ticket

Upload your file and generate a ticket to put your part in the queue.

4. Pick it Up

Come claim your part at the lab or we can ship it to you!
